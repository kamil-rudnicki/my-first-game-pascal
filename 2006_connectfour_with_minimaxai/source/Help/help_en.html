Rules

    The game is played on a board with 7 columns and 6 rows, which is placed in a vertical position.

    The players have 21 discs each, distinguished by color. The green player starts. The players take turns in dropping discs in one of the non-full columns.

    The disc then occupies the lowest unoccupied square on that column.

    A player wins by placing four of their own discs consecutively in a line (row, column or diagonal), which ends the game. The game ends in a draw if the board is filled completely without any player winning.
